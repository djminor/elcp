Welcome to the Endangered Language Conservation Project!

Home to the consolidated dictionaries of some of the most at-risk languages of the world!

  • Home
  • History
  • Future

Though not fully functional as of right now, this website will serve initially as a library of dictionaries, each one dedicated to preserving a different language.


Each of the languages contained within the ELCP have less than 10 million speakers worldwide, and many of them are not even included in Google Translate!

  • Ponca
  • Bathari
  • Boruca
  • Gottscheerish
  • Karaim
  • Tsakonian
